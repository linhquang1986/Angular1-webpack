HISTORY

Since 1983, when the first THX Certified home theater products were released, THX Engineers have been conducting thousands of tests on amplifiers, pre-amplifiers and loudspeakers. In 2006, the first projectors were Certified and in 2008 the first high performance televisions were THX Certified to meet the strict performance standards required to faithfully reproduce movies the way the director intended.

THX now uses this experience to objectively test and evaluate products to provide unbiased measurement data and metrics to help guide TV and Audio buying decisions. The program is being launched with products in the following categories:

TV
POWER AMPLIFIER
POWERED STUDIO MONITOR
PC SPEAKER

Additional products and new categories will continue to be added in an effort to provide a comprehensive database of measurements on the most popular AV products.

DEEP NOTE

Anyone who has been in a cinema, car or a live event knows the THX Deep Note. It is the deeply resonant chord that plays at the start of a THX-certified performance. Along with the THX logo, truest expression of the artist’s vision.

THX Deep Note was composed by Lucasfilm sound engineer Dr. James ‘Andy’ Moorer and was screened at the start of the 1983 premiere of Star Wars: Episode VI – Return of the Jedi.

In 2015 Dr Moorer refreshed the THX Deep Note and made it even more spellbinding for modern cinema formats. It remains one of the most recognized sonic identities in the world.
